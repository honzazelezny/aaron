We prepare it honestly and with love!

we are not the only ones, but we are special

We are a shop focused on the production and sale of kebabs. Our main specialty is a full range of kebabs - from quality meat to fresh ingredients and homemade sauces. All our products are made from fresh, high-quality and natural ingredients with an emphasis on taste and quality.


Our menu includes a number of different types of kebabs, from the traditional full kebab to vegetarian and vegan versions. All kebabs are served with fresh vegetables and our favorite homemade sauces.

Our kitchen is equipped with modern appliances and consists of qualified chefs with more than 8 years of experience in the field of gastronomy. All our products are carefully prepared by hand and are fresh and nutritious.
In our store, you can always be sure that you will get the best possible quality, both in terms of raw materials and production. And of course, our customers can always expect excellent service, a friendly attitude and a comfortable shopping environment.
So, if you're in the mood for a tasty kebab, come and see us!
